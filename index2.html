About Us

Lumos Development Studio

Lumos is a full-cycle digital studio focused on delivering results. We help businesses grow through high-quality websites, modern web applications, intuitive interfaces, and powerful mobile apps.

Our team consists of senior-level specialists with 10+ years of experience in web development, UI/UX, mobile engineering, and visual design. Even though Lumos is a young brand, our expertise is proven by years of working on complex, real-world projects across different industries.

Whether you need a sleek one-page website or a large-scale enterprise platform with custom architecture, integrations, and unique business logic — we can build it. We handle projects of any size and any level of complexity.

We work with a strong and future-ready technology stack: Python & Django, PHP & Laravel, React, Next.js Vue.js, Nuxt.js & React Native. This allows us to create fast, secure, scalable products that perform perfectly on any device.

Why clients choose Lumos: We deliver modern, world-class designs We build clean, reliable, and scalable code We communicate clearly and take full ownership We focus on results that bring real value to your business At Lumos, we don’t just execute tasks — we become your technology partner. Your goals become our goals, and we bring your ideas to life with precision, creativity, and passion. If you want a digital product that stands out and drives success — Lumos is ready.
